## Topics

1. Fully understand the growth requirements of different microorganisms  
2. Fully understand the techniques used to grow microorganisms under laboratory conditions 

1. Understand the evolution of microorganisms within the context of evolution of the Earth 
2. Understand “Endosymbiotic Theory” resulted in current microbial diversity • 
3. Understand Prokaryotic and Eukaryotic diversity 

1. What major geological events influenced the evolution of early Earth, providing a suitable environment for the emergence of life and subsequent diversification of microorganisms?
2. According to the Endosymbiotic Theory, how did ancient prokaryotes contribute to the formation of modern eukaryotic cells and what implications does this have on current microbial diversity? 
3. How do key differences in cell structures between Prokaryotes (Bacteria and Archaea) and Eukaryotes impact their overall functions, metabolism, and evolution within the biosphere?
