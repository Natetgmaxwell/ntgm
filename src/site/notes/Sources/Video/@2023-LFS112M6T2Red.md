# Notes  
### [[Cards/Red Blood Cells\|Red Blood Cells]]

- ~7μm diameter
- Haematocrit = % of RBC in whole blood
- Haemoglobin
    
    - Red pigment from Haemoglobin gived blood its colour
    - Binds and transports Oxygen
- Shape of RBC = Biconcave disc
- Features = flexible, stackable and no organelles
- Average lifespan of a RBC is ~120 days
- Haematopoiesis = making of formed elements in the blood; occurring in Red bone marrow.
    
    - Stimulated by Erythropoietin (EPO)
        
        - Released by Kidneys.
