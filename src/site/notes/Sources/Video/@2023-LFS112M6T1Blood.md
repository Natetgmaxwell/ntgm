# Notes  
### Functions of Blood

- Transportation of;
    
    - Dissolved gases
    - Nutrients
    - Hormones
    - Metabolic Waste
    - Antibodies
    - Clotting Factors

### Components of [[Cards/Blood\|Blood]]

- [[Cards/Red Blood Cells\|Red Blood Cells]]
    - 37-54%
- [[Cards/White Blood Cells\|White Blood Cells]]
    - Immunity
    - Have a [[Cards/Nucleus\|nucleus]]
    - have [[Cards/Organelles\|organelles]]
    - Types of WBC:
        - Neutrophils
        - Eosinophils
        - Basophils
        - Monocytes
        - [[Cards/Lymphocytes\|Lymphocytes]]
- [[Cards/Blood Plasma\|Plasma]]
    - 55%

### Serum vs [[Cards/Blood Plasma\|Plasma]]

- Serum is the portion of the blood that does not clot, therefore does not contain clotting protiens..
