# Notes  
### Digestive Organs

- Oral Cavity
    
    - Sensory analysis of food
    - [[Cards/Mechanical Digestion\|Mechanical Digestion]]
    - Lubrication
    - Limited chemical digestion of carbohydrates
        
        - Saliva
- Stomach
    
    - Storage of ingested products
    - Mechanical & Chemical Digestion
        
        - Gastric glands
            
            - Gastric juice
- Small Intestine
    
    - Parts
        
        - Duodenum
        - [[Cards/Jejunum\|Jejunum]]
        - Ileum
    - Chemical Digestion
    - Absorption
- [[Cards/Large Intestine\|Large Intestine]]
    
    - Storage of Digested waste
    - Fermentation and Vitamin synthesis.
